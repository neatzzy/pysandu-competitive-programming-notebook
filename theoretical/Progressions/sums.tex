\subsection{Sums}

Sum of first $n$ natural numbers: $\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$
Sum of squares of first $n$ natural numbers: $\dfrac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$
Sum of cubes of first $n$ natural numbers: $\left( \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} \right)^2$

Sum of squares of first $n$ odd numbers: $\dfrac{n(4n^2-1)}{3}$
Sum of squares of first $n$ even numbers: $\dfrac{2n(n+1)(2n+1)}{3}$
