\subsection{Arithmetic Progression}

A sequence where each term differs from the previous by a constant \textbf{common difference} $r$: $a_1,\, a_1+r,\, a_1+2r,\, \ldots$

\textbf{General Term:} $a_n = a_1 + (n-1)r$

\textbf{Sum of first $n$ terms:}
\[ S_n = \frac{n}{2}(a_1 + a_n) = n\,a_1 + \frac{n(n-1)}{2}\,r \]

\textbf{Special case} ($a_1 = 1$, $r = 1$):
\[ \sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \]
